About

Kenny Stephens is a web developer based in Olympia, Washington. He is currently working for Xactware helping to improve brand awareness on verisk.com. Some of his projects include migrations, features/enhancements, and bug fixes along with typical site and systems maintenance tasks.

Throughout his education and career, he has developed a keen sense for problem solving and has learned to thrive in team based environments. Kenny obtained his Bachelor's degree in web development from UVU, graduating summa cum laude. He is always looking to broaden his programming skill set and is excited to face his next challenge.
